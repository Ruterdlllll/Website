Our story

A family, two countries,
one love of fine stones.

Ipê began at a kitchen table across two continents. We are family: one of us in the Netherlands, one at the source in Brazil. Together we bring the country's gold, silver and gemstones straight to you — with nothing lost, and no one marked up, in between.

Sofia · Amsterdam
Co-founder · Curation & Care

From the Netherlands, Sofia curates each collection and looks after every order — from gift wrapping to lifetime repairs — so the whole world can wear Brazil.

Mateus · Minas Gerais
Co-founder · Sourcing & Craft

In Brazil, Mateus works hand-in-hand with local miners and goldsmiths, choosing every stone himself. What he sends north is only what he'd keep for family.

Direct sourcing

Fair prices

Cutting out the middle means honest pricing, from an €89 silver band to rare stones.

Real craftsmanship

Made by hand by people we know by name, and built to be worn for a lifetime.
